ARTIST PROFILE

Johannesburg born musician Joel 'Torpedo' Mathe is a young contemporary electronic dance music act and "modern day‚Äù composer who sets about on breaking boundaries and reaching people all around the world through music. Growing up with a deep passion for house music Torpedo began his journey in music production around 2017 experimenting with digital audio workstations and mixing software, he then continued learning more in depth about melodics and harmony, sound engineering techniques, live performing, and digital synthesis with the guide of industry professionals. He uses these techniques to create a progressive, melodic, afro inspired style of techno with the use of traditional instruments such as pianos, strings, and percussions, linear harmony, digital synthesizers as well as generative synthesis. It all comes together to provide an ambient, emotional, and uplifting experience for the listener. Torpedo's music career is set to begin in 2022 with a catalogue of forthcoming official debut releases and more performances. Torpedo's hopes are to release music on some of the most influential labels not only locally but internationally. His vision is to represent African electronic music in dance music scenes all around the world with a sound that is slightly different in many ways but still encapsulates house music as we know it.
